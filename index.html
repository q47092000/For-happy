For my Happy Wife

Before we begin, I want to thank you because you make every day wonderful for me.

Sometimes, I may not be very smart, especially if you suddenly ask me how my feelings

for you have changed over time. I worry that if my answer is too simple, you might think

I’m being perfunctory. And if I answer too quickly, it might seem careless. So, I take a

little time to think about how to respond properly. Thank you for patiently giving me that

time.



When I first got to know you, I witnessed our mutual craziness. But over these past days,

you’ve shown me your adorable side. 💕 For example, sometimes a part of you blushes

or feels shy 😍, but another part of you really wants to do it anyway. 😈 I love both sides

of you. Even though you sometimes worry about many things, I believe it’s because you

think a lot. There are times when you consider things I wouldn’t have thought of, and I’m

really happy that you’re willing to share your worries with me. But you can always rest

assured—I will help you. And when you’re hesitant, I’ll decisively give you advice

(even though the gift issue has been dragging on for a while 😂).



I know we both have our busy times, but no matter how busy I am, I will always think of

you. And when I’m caught up in work, I know that there’s someone out there thinking

about me, worrying about me, and silently supporting me. I’m so grateful to have such a

person in my life. Sometimes, I really wish I could be like a student again—fearlessly

running toward you, leaving everything behind to stay with you 24/7, or even flying

straight to Poland. :) But if I did that so soon, I think I might scare you away! 😂



By the way, do you like necklaces or earrings more? In my dream, I want to be by your

side every day, helping you fix your hair and dress up. :) (P.S. If I accidentally make you

look like a witch, don’t blame me! 🧙‍♀️ But don’t worry, I’ll be a wizard with you!)
